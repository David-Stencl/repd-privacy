REPD — Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

REPD ("we", "us", the "app") is a fitness app that lets you log workouts, track nutrition, analyze food and physique photos with AI, and share progress with a community. This policy explains what we collect, why, and your choices. By using REPD you agree to this policy.

Information you provide

  • Account: your email address and a username (used to sign in and identify you).
  • Profile: optional details you enter — country, sex, height, body weight, bio, weekly goal, training goal, and a profile photo.
  • Training & nutrition data: the workouts, exercises, sets, body measurements, meals, and drinks you log.
  • Photos: photos you take of food and drinks (for calorie analysis), physique photos (for body-composition analysis), and any photos you attach to community posts.
  • Community content: posts, comments, likes, and who you follow.

Information collected automatically

  • Location: only when you open the "Find a Gym" feature, to show gyms near you. We do not track your location in the background.
  • Apple Health (optional): if you grant permission, we read steps, active calories, and resting heart rate to show your activity, and can write weight entries you log. This is only accessed with your explicit permission and can be revoked in iOS Settings.
  • Diagnostics: crash reports and basic technical data (via Sentry) to find and fix bugs. These do not include your health data or photos.

How we use your information

  • To provide the app: save your workouts and nutrition, calculate stats, and sync across your devices.
  • To power AI features: photos and food/drink descriptions are sent to our AI provider to return nutrition estimates and physique analysis.
  • To personalize your experience: coaching messages, recommendations, and your weekly recap.
  • To operate the community: display your posts and profile to other users according to your privacy setting.
  • To keep the service safe: review reported content and enforce our terms.

AI processing

When you use the food, drink, or physique scanner, the relevant photo and text are sent to our AI provider (Anthropic) to generate the analysis returned to you. This processing is used only to provide the feature; it is not used to build a profile of you for advertising.

Service providers we share data with

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with providers that help us run the app:

  • Supabase — secure hosting, database, and photo storage.
  • Anthropic — AI analysis of food, drink, and physique photos.
  • Sentry — crash and error reporting.
  • Apple — app distribution and, if enabled, HealthKit data on your device.

Your physique photos

Physique photos are stored in a private location that only your account can access. Other users and the public cannot see them. You can delete them at any time.

Data retention and deletion

We keep your data while your account is active. You can delete your account at any time from Settings, which removes your personal data from our systems. You can also remove individual workouts, meals, drinks, photos, and posts within the app.

Security

Data is encrypted in transit, access is restricted per-user by database security rules, and your email address is stored only in our authentication system — never exposed to other users.

Children

REPD is not directed to children under 13 and, because it includes an alcohol-tracking feature, is intended for a mature audience per its App Store age rating. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

Your rights

You can access, correct, or delete your information from within the app, or by contacting us. Depending on where you live, you may have additional rights under laws such as the GDPR or CCPA; contact us to exercise them.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the app evolves. Material changes will be reflected here with a new "Last updated" date.
